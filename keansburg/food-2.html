Restaurants & Eateries

No trip to the boardwalk is complete without traditional boardwalk fare. Tempt your taste buds with everything from seafood to zeppole's.

Nickerson’s Ice Cream

Nickerson’s Ice cream has been serving freshly made hard ice cream since 1946 . Choose a cone, sundae,milkshake, or our crowd favorite; a famous waffle and ice cream sandwich! The combinations are endless with toppings like hot fudge, caramel, sprinkles, and more! 

Cheesy’s Pizza: OPEN UNDER NEW OWNERSHIP 2021

Craving some traditional boardwalk pizza? Cheesy’s is the place for you! Toppings include pepperoni, sausage, peppers, onions, and more! Traditional, Sicilian, or garlic knots! Yum!

CJ’s Spot

Funnel cake & fun traditional Jersey Shore boardwalk food!

Girardi’s Kabobs

Our original Fresh Grilled 1/4lb. Pork & Chicken Kabobs were established here in the park in 1997.
Also serving traditional Sabrett Hotdogs and other boardwalk favorites.

Dippin’ Dots

Not your traditional scoop, Dippin Dots is the cool way to enjoy an old favorite. Enjoy several unique flavors of this delicious dot delicacy!

Coastal Ice Cream

Enjoy a boardwalk favorite! Traditional Soft Served Ice Cream! Top off your treat with one of our several delicious toppings or order a Sundae, Thick-Shake, Slushie or Frozen Yogurt!

Pizza V

A boardwalk tradition, Pizza V serves classic thin crust Jersey pizza!

Nickersons

This open air eatery has all of your classic boardwalk favorites from sausage sandwiches and cheesesteaks to hamburgers and hotdogs. You can find something for everyone! And don’t forget to add some delicious cheese fries to your order.

Snowie Hawaiian Shaved Ice

A cool treat on a hot summer day, Snowie shaved ice comes in fun flavors to go!

Miami Club

Serving up the best seafood, the Miami Club offers a raw bar, seafood, burgers and the best homemade clam chowder in the Bayshore. Stop in for a Sugar Island rum bucket or our twist on the traditional Rum & Coke, an Island & Coke. The Miami Club offers a clean friendly sit-down atmosphere to order up food and drinks.

Heidelberg – Now Open!!

Established in 1934, enjoy one of Keansburg Amusement Park's oldest eateries! Famed hot dogs are again available along with German beer!

Sam’s Snack Shack

“Short and steep” is the idea behind this slide duo. Anyone who loves a thrill will enjoy the ride as they zip down feet first and drop into the splashdown pool.

Must be 42″ or taller. Under 48″ must wear a life jacket. Single riders only. Splash down pool in 10 ft.  All non-swimmers and novice swimmers are required to wear a life jacket.   Under 42" cannot ride. 

Glenda’s Great Fries

Warming pool located beneath Mega Bunga Falls where you can take a short respite.

Under 42" must wear a life jacket.

Mambo Nando’s : NEW 2021

Mambo Nando's Puerto Rican American cuisine.

Zeppole’s

The absolute best zeppole on the Jersey Shore! Freshly made daily.

Cotton Candy

Serving Cotton Candy, Fresh Lemonade, Churros, Pretzels and Refreshments!

Boardwalk Grill at the Pavilion Bar

Boardwalk Grill at the Pavilion Bar

Flavor Burst

Splash pool from 0″ to 12″ of water with all interactive water play. Short slides that sit next to each. Half the fun is climbing and playing your way to the top before sliding down.
